why do you live this life?

your piles of overworked paper cuts
the redness of your flooded brain
and the soreness of your clenched hands

meet me under this tree

let's watch the films with scenes that last too long
let's listen to the felted pianos with harmonies that are dissonant
and let's read the books with words that are disagreeable

I invite you to bare the life around us

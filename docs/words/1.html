Ode to Mints
you feature a large cranium
allowing for a gorgeous aesthetic eye
to paint the canvas of my mouth, gently;
I'm perpetually comforted in envy

although the physical remnants
of the chaos of this world remain,
your subtle intellectual gestures
rescue me through evoking a reverie

the reverie speaks, beautifully and passively:
(you are seen, you are loved,
and you are kissed in every breath to come);
then, anticlimactically, the reverie silences

due back on earth, I've come to sense how
your delicate touch of the tongue
and determined acceptance of life
agitates my heart

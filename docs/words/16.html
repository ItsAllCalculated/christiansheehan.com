The mistake that many composers make
The mistake that many composers make
is not making enough mistakes.
Let me ask you this:
Why are you so afraid of dissonance?
I like your piece,
but let's take a look at its melodic scale.
Why don't we flatten that third?
or maybe even that sixth?
Why don't you take the dominant 7th chord
in your 2-5-1 progression and sharpen that fifth?
I agree that your music has a pretty face,
but maybe a pimple or a scar will give it more charm.
Now, there we have it.
And don't fix it!
It's better now because it's more wrong,
and let's be honest with ourselves.
We all like to do things that are wrong.
